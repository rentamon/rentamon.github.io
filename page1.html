A Game Character Development Overview

Creation

The character had to exist under its size constraints of 64x64 px. He had to be readable in a detail-deprived style of art.

Conception

Described as a masked, rogue Hindu hero. Early ideas involved him in a golden mask, sleeveless armor, and typical old-age trousers.

Correction

Client feedback gave me essential details about the character that greatly affected his final persona.

Conclusion

The result was an original character based on Hindu inspirations.
